About

Talk UX is a platform for you to access to great speakers, great content, some fun, interactive sessions and new tech . It’s hosted by Ladies that UX, an international community of creative, inspiring women who work in UX design. In 2017, Talk UX will be held in Taipei, Taiwan . We would love you to join us at this fantastic event.
